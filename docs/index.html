An easier way to find good shows on the weekend: I follow music, but the bands I love don't always come through town – and I've often never heard of the bands that do play the small rooms near me. So this reads each venue's upcoming calendar and turns it into a Spotify playlist, refreshed daily. The idea came from a job candidate's coding-bootcamp project, built before agentic engineering – I hadn't seen anyone do it since, so here it is.
